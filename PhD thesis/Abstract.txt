In today's world, energy technologies have become indispensable,
playing a pivotal role across a multitude of sectors, 
with the Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) emerging as a cornerstone 
and catalyst for the global energy transition. Despite their immense importance, 
the design, construction, and operation of a BESS present formidable challenges, 
influenced by a myriad of user-specific criteria, project demands, and needs. 
Consequently, vigilant monitoring of BESS performance is not merely advisable 
but essential, ensuring both operational reliability and minimization of costly downtime.
