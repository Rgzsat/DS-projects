The European Association for Storage of Energy (EASE) 
is proud to launch its ninth annual Student Award recognising 
outstanding graduate student research in the field of energy storage.

The research must focus on an energy storage-related topic. 
Eligible papers are Bachelor's, Master's or Doctorates research papers 
submitted during the above-mentioned period or ongoing. 
EASE will be able to evaluate papers written in English
