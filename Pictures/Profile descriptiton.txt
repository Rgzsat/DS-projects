Dr. Rolando Antonio Gilbert Zequera work focuses on the fields of AI and energy technologies.
He graduated from TEC de Monterrey, Campus Monterrey with bachelor's in Engineering Physics in December 2018, 
master's in Engineering and Applied Sciences from MINES PArisTech in October 2021, and doctoral degrees in
AI and Mechatronics from Tallinn University of Technology in September 2025.

Dr. Rolando has prestigious education and international professional experience in the USA,
France, Norway, Germany, and Estonia. He is the inventor of a patent-pending technology with 
strong potential in battery systems and energy storage optimization
